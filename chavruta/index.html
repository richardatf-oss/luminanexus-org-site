Experiment in Torah & AI

ChavrutaGPT: a small AI learning partner.

ChavrutaGPT is an experimental tool from LuminaNexus.org. It cannot replace a teacher, rav, or real chavruta—but it can help you frame questions, explore ideas, and connect sources with care and curiosity.

Please do not treat responses as psak halacha, or as medical, legal, or financial advice. Always check with real-world teachers and experts.

Good ways to use ChavrutaGPT

  • Summarize a sugya or perek in simple language.
  • Compare classic mefarshim on a pasuk.
  • Ask for metaphors that connect Torah and physics.
  • Draft questions to bring to a live chavruta or rav.

Limits & boundaries

  • No real psak halacha.
  • May make mistakes or oversimplify sources.
  • Always check primary texts and teachers.
