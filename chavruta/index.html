How this chavruta works

ChavrutaGPT is designed to feel like sitting with a learning partner under the Quantum Etz Chaim. It can:

  • Help you summarize a sugya or perek in simple language.
  • Compare classic mefarshim on a pasuk or topic (Rashi, Ramban, Sforno, Netziv, etc.).
  • Suggest metaphors that connect Torah, science, and daily life.
  • Help you draft questions and ideas to bring to a live chavruta or rav.

Responses are generated by AI. Treat them as starting points for learning, not as final answers.

Good ways to use ChavrutaGPT

  • Summarize a sugya or perek in clear, simple language.
  • Ask how different mefarshim read the same verse or concept.
  • Explore how ideas in Kabbalah or Chassidus might echo modern physics (without overclaiming).
  • Draft divrei Torah, questions, or shiur outlines that you’ll later refine with real teachers.

Limits & boundaries

  • No psak halacha.
  • May miss nuances or reflect only some shittot or minhagim.
  • Sources may be paraphrased; always check the original sefer.
  • For life decisions (medical, legal, financial, or halachic), consult qualified humans.
