ChavrutaGPT · Alpha

A small AI chavruta to help you ask better questions.

ChavrutaGPT is an experimental tool from LuminaNexus.org. It cannot replace a rebbe, rav, or learning partner, but it can help you explore sources, frame questions, and connect ideas in a gentle, curious way.

Shalom! I’m ChavrutaGPT, a small AI learning partner created through LuminaNexus. Tell me what you’d like to explore—Torah, midrash, Kabbalah, philosophy, or just a big question that’s on your heart.

Please do not treat responses as psak halacha or medical, legal, or financial advice. Always consult real-world experts and rabbanim.

Good ways to use ChavrutaGPT

  • Summarize a sugya or perek in simple language.
  • Compare classic mefarshim on a pasuk.
  • Ask for metaphors that connect Torah and physics.
  • Draft questions to bring to a live chavruta or rav.

Limits & boundaries

  • No real psak halacha.
  • May make mistakes or oversimplify sources.
  • Always check primary texts and teachers.
