Chavruta Study Partner

Doorway

A patient, reflective AI chavruta rooted in Torah, Kabbalah, and careful questioning — never to replace human teachers, only to help you sit longer with the text.

This page is the gateway for your AI chavruta. In time, this space will open into a live conversation room where you can bring a pasuk, a sugya, or a question of heart, and explore it gently with structured prompts.

The chavruta here will not give psak or issue rulings. Instead, it will offer sources, questions, and perspectives, and will encourage you to bring halakhic questions to a qualified rabbi.

What this chavruta will do

  • Walk through a text line by line, in Hebrew and English.
  • Ask you gentle questions rather than rush to answers.
  • Offer background from Torah, Midrash, and classic sources.
  • Help you collect notes and questions for a human teacher.

For now, the wiring is still being prepared. When the time is right, this doorway will open into that live chavruta experience — alef by alef, sugya by sugya.
