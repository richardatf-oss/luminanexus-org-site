Chavruta Study Partner

Beta

A patient, reflective AI chavruta rooted in Torah, Kabbalah, and careful questioning — never to replace human teachers, only to help you sit longer with the text.

How to use this doorway

Bring a verse, a teaching, or a question of heart. Choose a mode, and speak to your chavruta as you would to a study partner. The chavruta will respond with questions, reflections, and sources to explore.

  • Study a text: Paste a pasuk, a line of Mishnah, or a teaching.
  • Ask a question: Share something you’re wrestling with.
  • Reflect: Use the chavruta to help you articulate your thoughts.

This chavruta does not give halachic rulings or medical advice. For halachic questions, please ask a qualified rabbi. For health or mental health questions, please speak with a professional.
