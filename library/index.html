What is this Library?

A structured series of books and resources exploring the Etz Chaim as a quantum-field Tree: ten sefirot, twenty-two letters, and 231 Gates as interactions of one living Field.

One Field, many Trees

The Quantum Etz Chaim is the primordial Tree. Each “Tree of Origin, Stone, Snow, Mirrors, Water, Fire, and more” is a branch or mirror – a local pattern of that one Field.

22 Sefer-Trees

Each Hebrew letter becomes a master Tree-volume: Sefer Aleph, Sefer Bet, … Sefer Tav. Each book focuses on one aspect of the unified Tree and its associated Gates.

231 Gates

Letter-pairs become Gates: structured ways the Field looks at itself. Over time, each Gate can become a chapter, meditation, visualization, or experiment in consciousness.

Sefer א — Etz HaReishit (The Tree of Origin)

Aleph as the first tilt from Ein Sof into expression. The Quantum Etz Chaim as the One Excitation in the unified Field. Ten sefirot as modes of relation, 22 letters as directions of motion, 231 Gates as interactions.

Gimel as the mover between. The architecture of the 231 Gates, pathways through consciousness and configuration space.

Sefer ח — Etz HaEven (The Tree of Stone)
