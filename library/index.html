The Celestial Library of the 231 Gates

The Quantum Etz Chaim in book form.

The Library gathers long-form work around the Quantum Etz Chaim: Sefer Aleph, the Trees of Stone and Mirrors, and volumes that weave Kabbalah, physics, and story into one field of light.

This is a living outline. As manuscripts grow, each entry can link to PDFs, audio, or interactive pieces.

ס

Seferim as orbiting branches of the one Quantum Etz Chaim.

What lives in this Library

A forest of Sefer-Trees: 22 primary volumes aligned with the Hebrew letters, plus companion works like Rider of the Forgotten Star and Ivrit HaOr.

Sefer Aleph — Etz HaReishit

The Tree of Origin: Aleph as the first tilt from Ein Sof into expression. One Field, ten sefirot as modes, 22 letters as directions, 231 Gates as interactions.

Sefer Bet — Etz HaMar’ah

The Tree of Mirrors: Bet as the house, inside/outside, left/right. Reflection and reversal across the pillars of the Etz Chaim.

Sefer Gimel — Etz HaShe’arim

The Tree of Gates: Gimel as the go-between. Architecture of the 231 Gates as paths of consciousness and configuration space.
