The Celestial Library of 231 Gates

In preparation

The 231 Gates (שערים) are the letter-pairs of the Hebrew alef-bet: two letters meeting, each combining its own flavor of creation. This Library is a gentle, symbolic way to sit with those pairings — not as rigid doctrine, but as small windows for meditation, journaling, and prayer.

For now, this page offers a small **231 Gates Explorer (beta)** with a handful of gates. As the Library grows, more gates and sefarim will be added, along with audio meditations and star-map views.
