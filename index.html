Our mission

To create bridges between sacred text, modern tools, and human hearts—so that learning feels like standing under a living Tree of Light.

Guard the light

Preserve and share Torah-rooted wisdom in ways that honor tradition while speaking in a modern voice.

Build new vessels

Design apps, stories, and experiences that let people encounter holiness through beauty, play, and curiosity.

Heal through connection

Use technology as a nexus—linking people, projects, and places into one field of mutual support and repair.

Get involved

LuminaNexus.org is small and growing. If this work speaks to you, there are many ways to stand under the Tree.

Learn & share

Use the Library, try ChavrutaGPT, and bring these ideas into your own learning spaces. Share feedback, questions, and corrections.

Collaborate

Writers, rabbanim, educators, coders, artists, and curious humans are welcome. If you feel called to help shape a particular Tree, reach out.

Support the work

As the nonprofit formalizes, there will be ways to sponsor specific sefarim, tools, or scholarships. For now, the best support is encouragement and connection.

Contact

To connect about learning, collaboration, or support, you can start with a simple email.

Email

Send a note to luminanexus@yahoo.com with a bit about who you are and which Tree (or project) you’re most drawn to.

Future contact form
