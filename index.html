Our mission

To create bridges between sacred text, modern tools, and human hearts— so that learning feels like standing under a living Tree of Light.

Guard the light

Preserve and share Torah-rooted wisdom in ways that honor tradition while speaking in a modern voice.

Build new vessels

Design apps, stories, and experiences that let people encounter holiness through beauty, play, and curiosity.

Heal through connection

Use technology as a nexus—linking people, projects, and places into one field of mutual support and repair.

Three pillars of LuminaNexus

Every project lives at the intersection of learning, imagination, and accessible tools.

Sacred learning

Rooted in Tanakh, Midrash, Kabbalah, and the voices of teachers across generations, presented with care and reverence.

Creative worlds

Novels, sigils, visual art, music, and interactive stories that invite people to step inside living metaphors.

Accessible technology

Websites, apps, and tools that are simple to use, welcoming, and designed for real people—not just experts.

Current & emerging projects

A glimpse of the constellation of work orbiting the Quantum Etz Chaim. Some projects are live, some are in active development.

The Celestial Library of the 231 Gates

A long-form project exploring the Quantum Etz Chaim and the 231 Gates as living pathways of consciousness, story, and field-theory-meets-Kabbalah.

Status: In development

Ivrit HaOr

A sacred Hebrew learning experience integrating Biblical and modern Hebrew, gematria, Kabbalah, and the hidden letter אלף עולם, with audio, art, and meditation.

Status: Prototype phase

ChavrutaGPT

An AI-assisted chavruta companion to help learners ask better questions, explore sources, and stay rooted in tradition while using modern tools.

Status: R&D

Rider of the Forgotten Star

A Western-planetary romance series inspired by classic pulp adventure, dedicated to family, faith, and the quiet heroism of the human heart.

Status: Manuscript series in progress

Mountain-to-Melody

A creative tool that turns mountain silhouettes into waveforms and music, letting landscapes become living scores.

Status: Concept & early prototype

CryptoPulse (exploratory)

A responsible, research-oriented exploration of how transparent tools might help people navigate complex financial systems with more clarity and caution.

Status: Exploratory research
