The Quantum Etz Chaim

One primordial Tree at the center, with each project as an orbiting light: Trees of Origin, Stone, Mirrors, Snow, Water, Fire, Song, and more—branches of a single living Field.

Our mission

To create bridges between sacred text, modern tools, and human hearts— so that learning feels like standing under a living Tree of Light.

Guard the light

Preserve and share Torah-rooted wisdom in ways that honor tradition while speaking in a modern voice.

Build new vessels

Design apps, stories, and experiences that let people encounter holiness through beauty, play, and curiosity.

Heal through connection

Use technology as a nexus—linking people, projects, and places into one field of mutual support and repair.

Trees of the Quantum Etz Chaim

Each “Tree” is a way the single Field shows a different face: origin, reflection, stone, snow, water, fire, and song. Some are active projects, some are sefarim-in-waiting.

Etz HaReishit — Tree of Origin

Sefer Aleph: Aleph as Field, Tilt, and Bridge. A modern walk through the Ari, quantum fields, and the first shimmering of existence from Ein Sof into the Tree of Life.

Etz HaMar’ah — Tree of Mirrors

Etz HaSheleg — Tree of Snow

Etz HaMayim — Tree of Water
